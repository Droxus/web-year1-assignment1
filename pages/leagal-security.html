Legal and security

Legal and Copyright Considerations

To ensure legal compliance, I have taken care to follow copyright regulations for all media used on this site. Images sourced from Google Maps, Sheffield university's website official website, and icons from Flaticon have been appropriately credited. These sources provide content that I am permitted to use, and I've included relevant links and references for transparency. This helps to ensure I respect intellectual property rights and avoid any legal conflicts related to copyrighted material.

Website Security

In terms of security, I have implemented several basic yet important measures in my contact form. The form is configured with the required attribute for both the email and comment fields to ensure that necessary data is provided. I've also used aria-required and aria-label attributes for accessibility purposes, ensuring that users with disabilities can interact with the form correctly.

Additionally, the form uses enctype="text/plain" to ensure the data is submitted in a simple, non-technical format. While this approach is basic, it ensures that sensitive information like email addresses is handled securely by sending data directly to the designated email address and not storing it on the website itself.
