The diamond kitchen

Situated just next to computer room 5 in the Diamond, they offer famous Grill & Go burritos, delicious wraps and hot burrito bowls, cooked fresh and made to order.

Heartspace Café

Modern meets industrial at the Heartspace Café, featuring great coffee and a range of cakes, cookies and snacks, alongside our range of delicious hot food prepared daily.

Arts Tower Café

Home of the famous paternoster lift, the Arts Tower also boasts this excellent cafe, featuring a range of tasty treasures fresh out of the oven and served all day.

Jessop Café

Jessop Café features a wide range of hot drinks and food prepared in house, such as poached eggs and avocado for a delicious healthy breakfast, or cakes and pastries for a lighter afternoon snack.
